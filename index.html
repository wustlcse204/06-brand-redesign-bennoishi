Introduction

Founded in Baton Rouge, Louisiana on August 28, 1996, Canes is a fast-food restaurant known for its tasty chicken fingers! Starting with our founder, Todd Graves, we have expanded to around 3,100 employees at over 500 locations! We use canola oil to cook all of our chicken and fries.
